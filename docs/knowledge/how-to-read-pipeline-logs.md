# Reading Pipeline Logs
1. Go to your repo → Actions tab
2. Click the workflow run
3. Each job shows as a box — green=success, red=failed, blue=running
4. Click a job to see steps
5. Click a step to see full log output
6. The "Security Gate" job shows all scan results
7. The "Deploy" job shows deployment details
8. The "Notification" job shows who was notified
